Research Work

Challenges of Internet Inclusion in Baidoa, Somalia

Journal: Journal of Emerging Technologies and Innovative Research (JETIR)

Publication Date: July 2025

Summary

This paper investigates the primary obstacles to widespread internet adoption in Baidoa, Somalia. It explores a range of interconnected issues, including significant gaps in digital infrastructure, the high cost of data, and low levels of digital literacy among the population. The research provides data-driven insights and proposes strategic recommendations for government, NGOs, and private sector stakeholders to foster a more inclusive digital environment.
